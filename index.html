Project Pine Beetle

Casual Fan

A web app intended for the laziest of fans, who just want to see a cheap game nearby. The site displays a map of games (with filters for sports, times, and location). It also queries ticket prices, and the top headlines for the teams and players so casual fans can feel informed (enough). Go sports! Or, like, whatever.

Deploying soon! Please check back 1/25/19.

Stories of Change

As climates shift globally, members of communities all over the world are experiencing tangible changes right at home. This web application is intended to display interviews and stories of local change to give color to the human experience of climate change.

Still interviewing! Please check back 3/31/19.

About Me:
I'm a senior at Dartmouth College studying Economics and Quantitative Social Science. I hope to learn about as many different subjects as possible while I am at college, but I am particularly excited about predictive modeling and software engineering.
