About Me and About this Site

About This Site:
This website was built with a Bootstrap HTML framework, beginning from a template found at W3schools.com. I purchased the domain name for free as a student through Namecheap. For more information, please visit my GitHub repository.

About Me:
I'm a junior at Dartmouth College studying Economics and Quantitative Social Science. I hope to learn about as many different subjects as possible while I am at college, but I am particularly excited about predictive modeling, data analytics, and front-end development. When I started college, I was really interested in applied mathematics, studio art, and the intersection between the two. Today, I love digital arts both because they are fun and creative, and because they present a perfect combination of math and art.
In addition to school, I am involved in the Dartmouth Coffee Club, studio art, and endurance sports. In my free time, I love going rock climbing, sea kayaking, and tidepooling.

Why DALI?
I would be thrilled to work for DALI this spring because it would provide me a fantastic opportunity to get real project experience, a chance to get to work on projects with a team, and an environment with unparalleled mentorship and skills development resources. Through talking with people in the lab and working with College Pulse, I am convinced that working with DALI is the best choice for my personal development and that it would be crazy fun.
I hope that I have convinced you (DALI) "why me " too!
